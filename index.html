About Us

Roxana Quant is a start-up in the financial services sector with the aim of becoming the first quantitative hedge fund in Iran. At Roxana Quant, we utilize novel methods that integrate principles from physics and mathematics, as well as Artificial Intelligence (AI), to analyze markets for investment and risk management. Our location is in Velenjak at the Shahid Beheshti University Science and Technology Park.
